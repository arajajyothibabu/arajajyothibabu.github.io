  • How does a data driven mobile product manager diagnose the reasons for drop in funnel?
    What sort of qualitative and quantitative analysis would help him/her gather the right insights?
    Answer
    With Apxor - You identify a drop in the funnel, and narrow down the segment where it is prominent – users are subjected to Apxor analysis which pinpoints any problems that lead to the drop. All the issues encountered by the target group like stability (crashes and ANRs), user experience (responsiveness, animation smoothness, UI related issues…), functional issues will be reported along with technical reasons. Apxor also allows you to dig deep into individual sessions of each user where you can get a complete understanding of the user journey.

    How do you compare and understand what makes the users active and stay in the app compared to the ones who become inactive?
    Answer
    Apxor Path Navigation will help you to quickly investigate the efficiency of activation sequence by providing deeper insights into user behaviour flow. Reasons for people who uninstalled in the first week can be completely different from uninstallation of loyal users.
