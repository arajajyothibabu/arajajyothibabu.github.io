Retention Analysis

 Home / Features / Retention Analysis
A major goal for every app is retention - to monitor factors that affect retention and figure out solutions to increase it. To increase retention, a key area to focus on is uninstalls - how many users uninstalled and why? Apxor provides multi-dimensional insights into why users aren't engaged with the app and reasons for abandonment.

With Apxor's Retention Analysis, you get answers:

How many users returned to the app the next day? How about a week or a month?
How many uninstalled?
How many are active users?
How many are likely to return?

  • Retention Cohorts

  • Using retention cohorts, we segment users who returned to the app and started using it in a specified time frame. Retention cohorts let you monitor to see if retention is increasing over a period of time and most importantly, to measure how the changes you've implemented are impacting retention.

  • Uninstall Tracking

  • Apxor puts special emphasis on uninstall tracking to help you understand which users uninstalled the app, which ones are inactive and likely to uninstall, and what were the last few sessions recorded before a user uninstalled. This knowledge gives you the power to impact retention by making the needed changes.
