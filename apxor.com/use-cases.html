Understanding App Uninstalls

With majority of app uninstalls occurring after the very first sessions, success of an app depends on what happens in the first few seconds after installation. Apxor helps in understanding user behavior in first few - did the app meet their expectation? did they struggle with performing too many steps? were some features annoying? did something interfere in the experience in those first few seconds?

Apxor provides an in-depth understanding of all these underlying issues. By comparing active users vs. uninstalled users, our platform further provides analytics that can not only help you understand uninstalls but also prevent them.

An analysis of users who used the app for a while and then uninstalled gives a view into the last few sessions before the uninstall, giving a clue into what could have made them uninstall.

Optimizing App for Conversion

Apxor measures and analyzes factors that have the highest impact on conversions, such as launch time, responsiveness, and usability issues. Apxor helps in identifying root causes for user dissatisfaction along with ideal values. E.g., reducing responsiveness from 3.2 seconds to 2.3 has a significant impact on conversion, beyond which there is no impact. These kind of insights help engineering teams to focus on the right metrics to target. We identify the optimal values of these metrics to aim for, for best utilization of effort and resources.

New Version & Feature Analysis

Most apps release new versions frequently. As a product manager, you are constantly scrambling to figure out how the new version impacted retention, conversion and engagement? Which features had a positive impact and which ones had a negative effect? Apxor helps identify how a feature is being used along with its impact on user retention, conversion and engagement.
